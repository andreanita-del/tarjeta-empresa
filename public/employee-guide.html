Important tips
📸
Upload the receipt the same day. Blurry or dark photos won't be read correctly. Make sure the receipt is flat and well lit.
🔴
Red expenses draw the manager's attention. If you see a red expense, upload the receipt as soon as possible.
✏️
You can edit or delete an expense if you made a mistake. Tap the ✏️ or 🗑 icon next to the expense.
🔑
Keep your password safe. You'll need it every time you log in. If you forget it, contact your company.
